Gunpla, short for "Gundam plastic models," is a hobby that I occasionally spend time on. It revolves around building and customizing models based on the mobile suits from the various Gundam anime series. You do it by assembling model kits of various robots each with their own detailed designs and articulation.

They kits come in different grades, ranging from beginner-friendly high-grade and entry-grade models to my personal favorite highly detailed and complex real grades.

I personally enjoy them as It de-stressess me when I build them and the satisfaction I get when they look great finished. Just sit down, put some music on and relax for a couple of hours as your creation comes to life.
